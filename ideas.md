### game concept
The game is a topdown kitchen restaurant manager, the cooks receive an order and cook it.  
The dishes are varied and based on themes.  

"cooking game where you use cooks to cook things" - mkaiast my homie  

### ideas  
top down view like pokemon  
cooks use pathfinding  
cooks can be upgraded to improve time taken to do things  
different dishes n style (either multiple restaurants or players choice)  
be able to decorate the restaurant with objects  
have a home area where you are before starting a shift, where you can set chefs, check your menu, check stats, etc  
chefs have need stats (like energy or other needs)  

### shop/building ideas  
recruiting/hiring building  
recipe book shop  
ingriedents upgrade shop (supermarket)
